Readiness Check

Winchester and Salji Checklist

Before you submit, confirm your literature review meets these standards.

  • ☐  Organized around themes, not individual articles
  • ☐  Each paragraph advances an argument, not just describes a study
  • ☐  At least 10 peer reviewed sources cited
  • ☐  Sources are synthesized and connected, not summarized one by one
  • ☐  Final paragraph identifies the gap your study will address
  • ☐  APA 7th edition citations throughout
  • ☐  4 to 5 pages not counting references
  • ☐  Graduate level prose with clear transitions between sections
